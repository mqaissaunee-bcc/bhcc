NETW 112: Containers & Orchestration

Containerization with Podman & OpenShift

Instructor: TBD Code: NETW 112

Course Description

This course serves as preparation for the **Red Hat Certified Specialist in Containers (EX188)**. Students develop practical skills in building, managing, and troubleshooting containers using Podman, then advance to administering containerized applications on an OpenShift/Kubernetes cluster.

RHEL 9 Podman 4.x Kubernetes APIs OpenShift 4.14+

Learning Outcomes

Podman Mastery

Images, Volumes, Containerfiles, and Compose.

OpenShift Ops

Deployments, Services, Routes, and HPA.
